Project Vision

During the development of Bankwise, our team recognized the common challenge of managing multiple banking apps to control one's finances. We aimed to simplify the user experience by creating an app that serves as a banking portfolio, enabling users to manage their finances across multiple banking accounts from different banks in one place.
 
In addition to simplifying the user experience, Bankwise also aims to enhance financial literacy among its users. We understand that many people struggle to understand financial concepts and are often intimidated by financial jargon. Therefore, we have incorporated educational tools and resources within the app to help users develop their financial knowledge and make informed decisions. Our goal is to empower users with the skills and knowledge necessary to achieve their financial goals and improve their overall financial well-being. With Bankwise, users can not only manage their finances easily and conveniently but also gain the knowledge and confidence to take control of their financial future.

The Framework

To better understand how we would construct the core experience for Bankwise, we created a framework. This helped us focus more on the overall structure of the app and less on the specific features that we would implement later on. It also allowed us to identify key areas of the app, such as account aggregation, financial insights, and transaction management, so that we could design a cohesive user experience that meets the needs of our users. The framework also helped us communicate the overall structure of the app more clearly, which was crucial for ensuring that all team members had a shared understanding of the app's architecture. The Bankwise framework is a five-step process that focuses on creating a simplified user experience and providing a more efficient way of managing finances across multiple banking accounts.

The Solution

The solution we came up with is a mobile application that simplifies the process of managing multiple banking accounts by enabling users to add and manage various bank accounts, check their credit card statements, view their bank statements, and manage their finances through a single app without the need to log in to multiple banking apps.
 
The app was developed with a focus on simplicity, ease of use, and accessibility. Users can easily navigate through the app, add new bank accounts, and manage their finances with just a few taps. The app is designed to streamline the user's banking experience and make it more convenient, saving users time and effort. The app's functionality is designed to provide users with a simple and efficient way to manage their finances, which is especially important for those with multiple bank accounts from different banks.
 
With future developments in mind, Bankwise aims to expand its functionality and integrate it with multi-tool platforms, making it a one-stop-shop for managing finances across the world.

Obstacles

Developing Bankwise presented several challenges that our team had to overcome. One of the most significant challenges was ensuring that the app was secure and reliable. Since users would be managing their financial information and transactions through the app, it was critical to have robust security measures in place to protect user data and prevent fraud.
 
Another challenge was integrating with multiple banking systems, each with their own APIs and unique features. Our team had to ensure that the app could seamlessly connect to various banking systems and provide users with accurate and up-to-date information on their accounts.
 
Usability was also a significant challenge. We wanted the app to be intuitive and easy to use for a broad range of users, including those who may not be tech-savvy or familiar with financial apps. To achieve this, we conducted extensive user testing and design iterations to ensure that the app's interface was user-friendly and straightforward.
